Welcome

Latarence Butts
March 20, 2021

I think that this a great place to begin writing.  

As I jot down these stories that I’ve lived with, I’m fascinated at what my mind chooses to remember and how vividly it remember it. Understanding what was most profound to me and what growth phases were most critical gives me a deeper sense of self.

I'm posting these pieces here for a few reasons. I find that when I work often I can miss the opportunity to reflect and revisit. I hate to live in the past, but healthy reflection can bring learning and appreciation. It is my hope that writing will help me conserve.

The next reason that I've began to write here is to for clarity. Things are beginning to move at a very fast pace professionally and in life. As my perspectives as they evolve, these pieces will bring awareness and clarity (as much as possible) to the present as I communicate to you. In the future, they'll serve as a snapshot of the past.

Lastly, this blog is my way to develop my relationship with the world. I’ve long withheld my expression and opinion for the sake of maintaining image or the lack of an appropriate medium. 

So, I’ve selected this medium to tell stories, to share truth, and to provide insight. It is my wish that it be beneficial to you. As with true conversation, let us know each other better in this exchange.
